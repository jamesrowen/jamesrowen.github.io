about

 

Hi, I'm James, a software developer hailing from the mountains of the Sierra Nevada and currently living in Austin, TX. I work at Accruent, coding on an enterprise SaaS product providing mission-critical support to the wireless industry. My interests include javascript (node.js, angularJS, etc), modern web development, software architecture, tools, artificial intelligence, and game development.

projects

 

My site  -  source

This site is a static page of handwritten HTML/CSS/JS, striving to incorporate modern best practices. It is constantly evolving as I learn, experiment, tweak, and embellish.

DICOMbinator  -   demo  -   source

A webapp for viewing and collaboratively annotating DICOM medical images. Runs on node.js with socket.io WebSockets for real time communication. I developed this at a hackathon with three strangers over 24 hours.

A 3D RTS/Sim game created with C#, XNA, and Blender. The player takes control of fledgling university, deciding which programs to build. Students controlled by the computer will register, learn to gain abilities, and fight for school pride.
